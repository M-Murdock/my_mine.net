About MyMine.net

Welcome to MyMine.net — a personal index of mine explorations, underground adventures, and historic mineral sites. This site is inspired by the great regional hiking databases and adapted for the world beneath the surface.

Each entry includes GPS coordinates for the trailhead or mine portal, notes on access and conditions, historical context, and personal observations from visits. Photos are added as available.

Mine exploration carries real hazards including poor air quality, unstable ground, flooding, and structural collapse. Always research conditions before entering any mine, bring proper equipment, and never explore alone.

This is a living database — updated as new mines are visited and new information is gathered.

⚠ Safety Disclaimer
Abandoned mines are dangerous. This site documents personal visits for informational purposes only. Do not trespass. Check local regulations. Always tell someone where you're going.
